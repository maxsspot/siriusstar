SIRIUS

INFO

Name: Sirius
Type: Average Star
Description: Brightest star in the night sky. It's name means glowing, and is located 8.6 light years away from Earth.


LIFE CYCLE

1) It is a stellar nebula. Stellar nebulas are giant clouds of dust and gas.

2) It now turns into an average star. An average star is a common type of star.

3) It now turns into a Red Giant. This is when hydrogen is depleted and it expands.

4) It now turns into a Planetary Nebula. This is an expanding, glowing shell of very hot gas.

5) It finally turns into a white dwarf. This is when the star is hot and dense.
